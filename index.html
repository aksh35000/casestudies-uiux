AK

Designing with purpose, curiosity, and craft

Hi, I'm a UI/UX designer with a background spanning enterprise SaaS, fashion e-commerce, and product startups. I believe great design is invisible — it simply feels right.

My process is rooted in research. I don't guess at what users need — I observe, map, test, and iterate. Whether it's a subscription page, a blog layout, or a full design system, every decision is traceable to a real insight.

I've had the opportunity to contribute to design at Iresh Technologies, Fashion Index, and Plasmid — helping ship subscription pages, about pages, blog pages, component libraries, and end-to-end product flows.
